Privacy Policy

Life In The UK Practice Tests ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require users to create an account or provide personal information to use the app.

The app may collect limited non-personal information automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data (such as progress tracking and test performance)
  • Anonymous analytics data to improve app performance

2. How We Use Information

Information collected is used only to:

  • Provide realistic practice tests and flashcards
  • Track user progress within the app
  • Improve app functionality and content accuracy
  • Fix bugs and enhance user experience

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal user data with third parties.

Anonymous analytics data may be processed by trusted services (such as Google Analytics for Firebase) solely for app improvement purposes.

4. Advertising

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may use cookies and other technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to websites and apps.

For more information about how Google uses data for advertising, please visit: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

5. Children's Privacy

Life In The UK Practice Tests is intended for users preparing for the UK citizenship test and is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information collected through the app. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is 100% secure.

7. User Consent for Advertising

When you first use the app, you will be asked to provide consent for personalized advertising. You can change your consent preferences at any time in the app settings.
