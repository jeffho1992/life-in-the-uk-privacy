Privacy Policy

Life In The UK Practice Tests is a mobile application developed and published by jeffho1992 (the "Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We do not require users to create an account or provide personal information to use the app. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

The app may collect limited non-personal information automatically, including:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App usage data (test progress, performance metrics, session duration)
  • Anonymous analytics data (crash reports, feature usage statistics)
  • Advertising identifier (for ad personalization with consent)

1.3 Sensitive Information

We do not collect any sensitive personal information as defined by applicable privacy laws, including but not limited to:

  • Racial or ethnic origin
  • Political opinions
  • Religious beliefs
  • Health information
  • Biometric data

2. How We Use Information

Information collected is used only to:

  • Provide realistic practice tests and flashcards
  • Track user progress within the app (stored locally on device)
  • Improve app functionality and content accuracy
  • Fix bugs and enhance user experience
  • Display relevant advertisements (with user consent)

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

3.1 Personal Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share personal user data with third parties. No personal information is shared with any external parties.

3.2 Non-Personal Data Sharing

Anonymous analytics data may be processed by the following trusted services:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: For app performance monitoring and crash reporting
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements (with user consent)

3.3 Data Transfers

All data transfers use secure encryption protocols. No data is transferred to countries without adequate privacy protection.

4. Advertising

This app displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may use cookies and other technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to websites and apps.

For more information about how Google uses data for advertising, please visit: How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services

You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ad Settings.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement the following security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Local data storage uses device-level encryption
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication for developer systems
  • Secure coding practices following OWASP guidelines

5.2 Data Breach Notification

In the unlikely event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

6. Data Retention and Deletion

6.1 Data Retention Period

  • Local App Data: Retained until user uninstalls the app or clears app data
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 26 months in Firebase Analytics
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days in Firebase Crashlytics
  • Advertising Data: Retained according to Google AdMob policies (typically 90 days)

6.2 User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data by clearing app data or uninstalling the app
  • Opt out of analytics and advertising tracking
  • Request a copy of any data we have about you

6.3 Deletion Process

To delete your data:

  • Go to Settings > Apps > Life In The UK Practice Tests > Clear Data
  • Or uninstall the app completely
  • For data deletion requests, contact us at jeffho1992@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

Life In The UK Practice Tests is intended for users preparing for the UK citizenship test and is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 13, please do not use this app.

8. User Consent for Advertising

When you first use the app, you will be asked to provide consent for personalized advertising. You can change your consent preferences at any time in the app settings or through your device's advertising settings.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored on servers located in the United States and other countries where Google operates data centers. All transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We will notify users of significant changes through the app.
